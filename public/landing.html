How it works

Register your venue

Sign up, name your space, and pick your brand colors. Takes 30 seconds.

Connect Spotify

Link your Spotify Premium account so songs play through your speakers.

Print the QR code

Download and display the auto-generated QR code at your tables or counter.

Guests pick songs

They scan, search, and add a song. It shows up in the queue and plays on your system.
