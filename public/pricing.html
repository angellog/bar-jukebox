Frequently asked questions

Do I need Spotify Premium?
Yes, the venue owner needs a Spotify Premium account to play full songs through the speakers. Guests do not need any Spotify account -- they just search and add.
Can guests install an app?
No app needed. Guests scan a QR code and land on a mobile-optimized web page. It works instantly in any browser.
How do I control what gets played?
You have full admin controls: skip songs, remove items from the queue, block specific tracks, filter explicit content, and set rate limits per guest.
Can I use my own branding?
On Starter plans and above, you can customize colors, logo, fonts, welcome message, and even inject custom CSS. Your guests see your brand, not ours.
What happens when the queue is empty?
When no songs are queued, the player simply waits. You can optionally set up a fallback playlist on your Spotify account.
